How DIF Protects You

Your digital identity fingerprint (DIF) is a cryptographic proof of authenticity.

1

Biometric Hashing

We analyze key facial vectors and convert them into a unique alphanumeric string (Hash). This process is one-way and irreversible.

2

Blockchain Anchoring

The hash is anchored to a public ledger. This creates an immutable timestamp that proves existence at a specific point in time.

3

Deepfake Detection

Any alteration to your content will generate a mismatched hash, instantly alerting our detection systems to potential manipulation.
